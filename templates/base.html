Jotly Usage Tips
  • Quick Toggle: Click on the icon of a Task (•) or Completed Task (x) to instantly change its status.
  • Hover Actions: Hover your mouse over any log entry (or tap on mobile) to reveal hidden actions like Edit (pencil), Migrate (>), or Schedule (<).
  • Immutability: Tasks that are already Migrated, Scheduled, or Completed cannot be edited further to preserve history.
  • Global Entry: Use the `+ Add Entry` button in the sidebar to add a new task or note to any day, from any page.
